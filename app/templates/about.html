About Mentor Connect

Mentor Connect is a specialized web application designed to support NGOs in managing their student and mentor programs.

Our platform facilitates the seamless connection between students seeking guidance and experienced mentors willing to share their knowledge and expertise.

Our Mission

To empower educational and community-focused NGOs by providing a robust, intuitive system for program administration, fostering effective mentorship relationships, and tracking student progress.

Key Features

  • Role-Based Access: Separate dashboards and functionalities for Admins, Mentors, and Students.
  • User Management: Admins can easily add, edit, and delete user accounts.
  • Mentor-Student Matching: Admins can assign students to mentors, centralizing relationship management.
  • Secure Authentication: User data and sessions are protected with industry-standard hashing and session management.
  • In-App Messaging: Private, secure communication between mentors and students.
  • Session Logging: Mentors can log session details and student progress.
  • Resource Library: Curated learning resources for students.
  • Announcements: Admins can post important updates for all users.
  • Basic Reporting: High-level overview of program statistics for admins.
